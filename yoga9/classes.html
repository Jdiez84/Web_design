Yoga Classes

Gental Hatha Yoga
Intended for beginners and anyone wishing a grounded foundation in the pratice of yoga, this 60 minute class of posses and slow movement focuses on asana (proper aligment and posture), pranayama (breath work), and guided meditaion to foster your mind and body connectinon.
Vinyasa Yoga
Although designed for intermediate to advanced students, beginners are welcome to sample this 60 minute class that focuses on breath-synchronized movement- you will inhale and exhale as you flow energetically through yoga poses.
Restorative Yoga
This 90 minute class features very slow movement and long poses that are supported by a chair or wall. This calming, restorative experiance is sutiable for students of any level of experiance. This pratice can be perfect way to help rehabilitate an injury.
